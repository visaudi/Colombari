Compagnia de' Colombari (Company Colombari) is a collective of performing artists that generates theater in surprising places. Colombari intentionally clashes cultures, traditions and art forms to bring fresh interpretation to the written word old and new.

Guided by the vision and leadership of Karin Coonrod, Artistic Director, Colombari believes that every public place holds the potential to be a space for the sacred architecture of theater. Colombari commits to using any means possible to flesh out the written word and make it heard and deeply felt. Incorporating the highest production values, the work of Colombari brings performers and audiences together, reflecting the time-honored tradition of transforming strangers into community.

Company Colombari is founded on the twin principles that the magic of great theater can happen anywhere and be made accessible to everyone.
